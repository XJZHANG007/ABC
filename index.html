Xuejing Zhang

I am currently an Associate Professor with the School of Information and Communication Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China.

Email: zhangxuejing@uestc.edu.cn

Research

I'm interested in array signal processing, tensor signal processing, convex optimization, integer programming, with their applications to radar and communications.
